What it does

Tinder for photos

Left to delete. Right to keep. iOS confirms before anything is removed.

Find the duplicates

Apple's Vision framework groups near-identical frames and visually similar shots — locally.

Burst best-pick

Pulls the sharpest frame from a burst. Sends the rest to the trash on your call.

Screenshot triage

Smart sorting by Foundation Models — receipts, memes, addresses — all on device.

Large videos

Surface every video over 100 MB. Preview, multi-select, free a gig in seconds.

Protected albums

Mark Wedding, Kids, ID Docs off-limits. Pocketsweep never shows them.
