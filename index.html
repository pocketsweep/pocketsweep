FAQ

Honest answers.

Does Pocketsweep upload my photos?

No. Every scan, feature print, OCR pass, and similarity comparison happens on the device. There is no server backend.

What happens when I "delete" a photo?

iOS shows its own confirmation, then moves the photo to Recently Deleted for 30 days. Pocketsweep never bypasses this. You can recover anything for a month.

Is the free tier actually usable?

Yes. 10 lifetime deletes with the full scanner unlocked. Most people figure out within those 10 if the app feels right.

Why $9.99 lifetime when others charge $4.99/week?

Because the work is done in a single sweep. Charging weekly forever for a one-time job is the trap. Pay once, own it.

What languages is it in?

16 fully translated locales today (Spanish, French, German, Italian, Brazilian Portuguese, Japanese, Mandarin, Cantonese, Korean, Arabic, Russian, Hindi, Turkish, Dutch, Polish, Indonesian) — plus 24 more falling back to English as we ship translations.
